OpenVAF at Analog Working Group

Posted March 21, 2023 by Markus Müller ‐ 1 min read

The analog working group of the chips alliance organization had a meeting on 21st. March 2023 where OpenVAF was presented. Legendary Ken Kundert (Spectre creator) and SemiMod (represented by Pascal Kuthe) gave presentations on OpenVAF and its benefits to the EDA ecosystem. After the talks, there was a productive discussion regarding the current state of Verilog-A and funding opportunities for OpenVAF. You can find a recording of the meeting and the presentations, here.
